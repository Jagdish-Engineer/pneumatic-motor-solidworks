# Pneumatic Motor Design & Simulation (SolidWorks)

## Overview
This project focuses on the design, assembly, and motion simulation of a pneumatic motor using SolidWorks. The objective was to understand the working principles of compressed air-driven systems and translate them into a functional mechanical model.

## Key Features
- Complete 3D CAD modeling of pneumatic motor components
- Assembly of rotor, housing, shaft, and supporting elements
- Motion simulation to visualize rotational output
- Design focused on smooth power transmission and mechanical stability

## Design Approach
The motor was designed by first modeling individual components such as the rotor and casing, followed by assembling them using proper mates and constraints. Emphasis was placed on maintaining alignment, minimizing frictional losses, and ensuring consistent rotational motion under simulated conditions.

## Tools & Software
- SolidWorks (Part Design, Assembly, Motion Study)

## Results
- Successfully achieved continuous rotational motion through simulation
- Validated the working principle of pneumatic energy conversion into mechanical motion
- Demonstrated effective component interaction and assembly design
